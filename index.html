Kanna Bump is joining Devlin Studios!

We are welcoming Kanna Bump to our range of bots. We will continue to develop the bot into a similar, but differentiated version of the most popular bump bot: ServerMate

Some general questions you may have...

So, what will happen to the bot?

Soon, the bot will come online with a practically identical version of ServerMate, with an hour long bump cooldown time and a slightly different looking bump message with each server.

Over time, the bot will be developed on much more and will be changed to act quite different to ServerMate, to pass as an alternative option, instead of seen as a "ServerMate 2" bot, which is absoloutely not the case.

Will it still be a bump bot?

Of course! We will not be changing the purpose of the bot, we will only be changing the way the bot functions slightly over time. But the bot will still be focused on bumping your server.

Will the bot's branding change?

While we are considering changing both the name and icon of Kanna Bump, we haven't quite got to the point of deciding that. We may take some feedback on this in the future and make our decision based off of the results of the poll

What is ServerMate?

ServerMate is the leading bump bot designed for discord. It bumps your server to around 200 servers each time you !bump your server. We manage the bot and often make changes to it that also influence other bump bots to change along with ServerMate, which we don't mind. We are hoping that this can be the same with our newly aquired bot: Kanna Bump!
